Origin

Senior year, I built a portfolio site before anyone told me to. That decision is the whole story.

I'm from Milwaukee, Wisconsin — self-taught in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a Microsoft IT Help Desk Pro certification earned before I ever sat in a college lecture hall. While most of my graduating class was finishing applications, I was already shipping code and rendering scenes in Blender.

This summer I started classes at Marquette University through the EOP-SSS program — a head start most freshmen don't get, and one I don't take lightly. I'm entering as Software Engineering, Class of 2030.

" I build first and figure things out along the way.

That's the throughline across everything here — the code, the renders, the brand. Nobody handed me a syllabus for any of it.
